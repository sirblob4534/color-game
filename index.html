I call this game "Risky Wordle".
The there are three things you have to know when playing.


1. You are unable to click backspace, this means every move you make needs to be carefully calculated and thought out.

2. the only way to restart is to refresh the page, BUT when you refresh the page, a new randomly chosen word is loaded. No second chance

3. There is no limit to what you can type in the boxes, but it has to be five letters long
